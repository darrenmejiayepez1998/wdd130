The 7 habits of highly effective people by Stephen Covey

There is no better book that is not considered scripture for my church that I would say has had more impact in my life than the 7 habits, I have read this book twice and will go over it again, It is just filled with applicable advise and it is based on principles that never change and is applicable to anything, this is a world famous book and there is not much I can say that has not already been say but I can give a strong recomendation that this is a book that will help you level up. In addition to that something I can add is how it has affected me, it has helped me see that there is much I can do to improve in my day to day by being more proactive, seeking to understand others before I ask for understanding, as well as putting the things that matter most first, I am not anywhere near to great in any of these things but I have been able to improve on all of these things and more because of this book.

Getting to yes by Roger Fisher and William Ury

This similar to 7 habits is a book that is based on eternal principles but more from a negotiation persoective, this is especially helpful for people that are getting into any of the fields that are in my portfolio or any field really since negotiation is something essential in any art or buisness. There is a few similar principals in getting to yes with 7 habits, one of those is seeking the "other side" of a negotiation as another person and seeking to understand There view on the matter, there are a lot of examples in the book about the importance of that principal.
