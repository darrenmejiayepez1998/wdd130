I am reading a lot of content that is meant for self-improvement a lot of these books are fairly well known but there are a lot of people my age or younger who do not know about these books or their value and this subpages purpose would be to compile the ones I have gone through and document why they are a good read and what people could get out of them, this sub page would include stuff like 7 habits of highly effective people and getting to Yes, but in the future once I get to more books I would also add them to the page, but for the sake of this project and the semester those would be the 2 main ones I would add there, I do not want this to be confused with a blog post but more as a helpful more in-depth review or assessment of why these books can be helpful for someone who is in similar circumstances as me, that be it that they are around my age or that that they have similar aspirations as me, the reason I think this would be valuable is because some of these books have true eternal principals that are helpful no matter what field you are in and more people my age should be aware of them and should apply them to there life so that they can reach there goals and aspirations, right now I am in my path to success and this website will help me document the things I am doing and that I will be doing in the future to make it happen.
